KIDS N HERS Makeup School & Saloon

Kids & Hers the most Comprehensive Professional Saloon was established in 9th August 2005. In South Kolkata it is the most advanced professional service available. For the last thirteen years we have established ourselves in all type of services i.e. Make-up, Hair and Beauty. Subsequently we have opened two branches in South Kolkata.
